# Empathy Style

Use when responding to a customer who is reporting a problem, frustration, or confusion.

## Procedure

1. Mirror the situation in one short sentence (no apology theater).
2. State what you know to be true based on lookup results.
3. State what you will do next, or what the customer should do next.
4. Stop. Do not pad.

## Constraints

- Do not say "I understand how you feel."
- Do not promise a timeline you cannot verify.
- Do not invent root cause. If unknown, say "I don't have a confirmed cause."
- Avoid "unfortunately" and "as per."
